About NYSL

Mission

To support young athletes living in Chicago's northside neighborhoods, who have an interest in learning and playing soccer, with opportinities to learn and practice skills ralated to the game of soccer, specifically those skills around team cooperation and good sportsmanship.

Vision

The Northside Youth Soccer League aspires to develop strog, well-rounded, and mindful athletes through the building of character, self-discipline, and leaddership.

General Information

The Northside Youth Soccer League was established in 1996 to provide athletes residing in Chicago's northside neighborhoods an enviroment in which to learn and play soccer. To be a member of NYSL, you must be between the ages of 4 - 12 and reside un a Chicago northside neighborhood. NYSL is ran by a small full-time staff, and rilies on the generous volunteer time of parents and previous league members.
